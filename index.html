About Us

Johar! Welcome to Enej Sereng – your joyful space to celebrate and discover the rich Santali culture. Learn Ol Chiki (the beautiful Santali script created by Pandit Raghunath Murmu), enjoy Enej (traditional Santali dances), feel the soul of Sereng (folk songs), explore Horo Bande (Santali lifestyle, traditional outfits, and way of life), along with delicious foods, vibrant festivals, singing, and many more traditions of the Santal people. Whether you want to read in Ol Chiki, dance to the rhythm of Baha Enej, or understand our Horo Bande, this blog shares authentic stories, knowledge, and pride in Santali heritage. Join us in preserving and spreading the spirit of our culture with love and respect! 🌼
